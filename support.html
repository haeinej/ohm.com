Contact support

For help with ohm., email support@ohmmmm.com. Please monitor this inbox before App Store submission so review and user support requests are received.

What we can help with

  • Login and account access issues
  • Account deletion requests
  • Profile or photo issues
  • Reply, conversation, or collaborative card problems
  • Privacy and data requests
  • Bug reports and app performance issues

Account deletion

If you want to remove your account, use Me > Settings > Delete Account in the app.
