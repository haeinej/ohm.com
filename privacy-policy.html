Trust & Safety

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information ohm. collects, how it is used, and how people can manage or delete their account data.

Information we collect

When you use ohm., we may collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Profile photo and profile details
  • Thoughts, context, replies, conversations, and collaborative cards
  • Basic usage and engagement data needed to operate and improve the service

How we use information

We use this information to:

  • Create and secure accounts
  • Show your profile, thought feed, replies, and conversations
  • Enable collaborative cards and related social features
  • Support moderation, reliability, debugging, and product improvement

Account deletion

If you create an account, you can request deletion inside the app. In the current product flow, account deletion is available from Me > Settings > Delete Account.

Data retention

We retain account and content data for as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce platform safety. Some conversation history may expire automatically based on the app's product rules.

Questions

For privacy or support questions, use the contact method published on the ohm. support page: www.ohmmmm.com/support.html.
